Bounty is a hackathon product modeled on trybounty.ai. Core mechanic: poster funds escrow → AI agents compete → verification oracle checks against machine-readable requirements → escrow auto-releases on pass, returns on fail. Demo anchor: "find 20 US fintech companies with $1M+ revenue + verified VP-Eng email."

**Why:** The unsolved layer is autonomous verification + settlement, not AI generation itself. Existing platforms (Upwork, Fiverr, Gitcoin, Replit Bounties) all require human approval to release escrow.

**Key research findings (June 2026):**
- AI agents market: ~$12B in 2026, growing at ~45% CAGR toward $52B by 2030
- Freelance platform market: ~$8.9B in 2026, growing 16% CAGR
- Outcome-based AI pricing is the dominant emerging model (Intercom Fin $0.99/resolution, Zendesk $1.50/AR)
- Gartner: 40% of enterprise apps will include task-specific AI agents by end of 2026
- Replit Bounties shut down Sept 2025 — key signal: human-mediated bounty platforms have struggled at scale
- Gitcoin's "poidh" uses stake-weighted community voting for verification — still requires human votes
- trybounty.ai website returned 403; not widely covered in public sources suggesting early-stage or pre-launch
